The Platform is an initiative of College which aims to provide some of the most necessary services availed by startups at very concessional rates compared to the market rates. The predominant problem faced by startups is that they fail to find a reliable and cheap vendor and end up paying manifold rates for the same. In case you want to avail any of the services, just click on the service tab and fill-up the form after which you will be shortly connected with the service provider.
Our Vision

As a non-profit student body aimed at promoting a healthy entrepreneurial ecosystem, Club strives to bridge the gap between the student start-ups and the capital intensive services and consultancies that a start-up needs in the early stage.

Our Mission

To facilitate this vision we have initiated the Platform wherein we aim to help such budding start-ups in accessing the full benefit of start-up needs and essential services at discounted rates.

What We Do?

We have initiated the Platform for start-ups in our network wherein we partner with firms that provide basic services required by startups to provide services at discounted rates.
